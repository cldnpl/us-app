Us. — Couple App

Two people, one little world.

Us. is a private app for couples: count your days together, send an "I miss you" from your home screen, keep a shared journal with photos, play quizzes and games together, and stay close even when you're far apart.

Common questions

How do I connect with my partner?
Create an account, then share your pairing code with your partner (or enter theirs).

How do I cancel Us. Premium?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Us. Premium → Cancel.

How do I delete my account?
In the app: Settings → Account → Delete account. Or email us and we'll take care of it.
